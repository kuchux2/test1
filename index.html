Gon Freeccs

Gon Freecss is the main protagonist of Hunter Ã— Hunter. He is a young and energetic boy from Whale Island who dreams of becoming a Hunter to find his father, Ging Freecss. Gon is known for his incredible determination, natural talent, and strong sense of justice. His Nen ability falls under Enhancement, giving him immense strength and resilience. Despite his kind and cheerful nature, he can become dangerously ruthless when provoked.

Killua Zoldyck

Killua Zoldyck is a skilled assassin from the Zoldyck family in Hunter x Hunter. He is intelligent, agile, and masters electricity-based Nen. Despite his dark past, he is loyal to his friends, especially Gon, and seeks his own path.

Kurapika Kurta

Kurapika Kurta is the last survivor of the Kurta Clan in Hunter x Hunter. He seeks revenge on the Phantom Troupe for massacring his people. A skilled Nen user, he wields chains and gains powerful abilities when his Scarlet Eyes activate. Despite his cold demeanor, he values justice and friendship.

Leorio Paradinight

Leorio Paradinight is a medical student and aspiring Hunter in Hunter x Hunter. He is driven by his desire to become a doctor and help others. Despite his lack of combat skills, he is intelligent, resourceful, and has a strong sense of justice. Leorio's Nen ability falls under Emission, allowing him to teleport objects through portals.
